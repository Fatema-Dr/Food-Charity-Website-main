Frequently Asked Questions

Is Doctor's Foudation a food bank?

Doctor's Foundation isn't a food bank. Food banks will provide items to be taken home to cook. We run Community Meals, which means we invite people to come and sit with us, around a table and enjoy a hot, nutritious meal. Our weekly community dining service not only helps tackle food poverty but it also helps alleviate loneliness by bringing the community together.

How do Doctor's Foundation source their food?

We work with supermarkets, small independent grocery shops and markets to source perfectly edible surplus food in a safe and responsible way. We only reclaim fresh fruit and vegetables and we purchase extra ingredients such as dried foods and spices to make our meals as balanced and nutritious as possible.
